2008 Northwest Crank
Thursday April 24 through Monday April 28, 2008

What is it?

Northwest Crank is a cycling festival to meet and ride with your fellow cyclists from around the state of Washington and the Northwest for some fine spring cycling in Wenatchee. Why should you join us?

    • A chance to meet riders from all over the state and region.
    • Build your base miles for the summer riding season.
    • Explore the wonderful roads around Wenatchee.
    • All you have to do is eat, sleep, and ride!
    • Finally get rid of some of your old bike parts in the White Elephant gift exchange.

One of the best reasons to join us is to build your spring base miles. There are numerous opportunities around the US during the spring to spend a week just riding. Most of these training weeks are to the south. Here’s your chance to explore your home region and get the same benefits. All those extra miles will help you maintain and build your endurance. The goal is just to finish each day - as you ride you’ll build strength and endurance and in a few weeks you’ll notice an improvement in your riding.

Each day has at several featured rides to choose from and these have support.  If you choose, you may of course ride anywhere you want, self-supported.

Don’t miss out on 2008 as the festival grows with more routes (including an adventure series), several contests, and of course more riders!

You can register here(link coming soon).

If you have any questions, please email us at info2008@northwestcrank.com.

When?

For 2008, Northwest Crank starts on Thursday April 24 and ends Monday April 28. Come for any or all of the 5 days!

Where?

NW Crank will once again be headquartered at The Inn At The River at 560 Valley Mall Parkway in East Wenatchee, WA.  Wenatchee is centrally located in Washington, less than a 3 hour drive from most of Washington.

Volunteer Opportunities For an event like this we all want to be out riding and enjoying the roads and the support. That support is critical is making this happen. We would appreciate your help! Maybe you’d like to take a day off in the middle. Perhaps a significant other is coming with you and could help for a few hours one day. Please let us know if you are interested and able to help. The more help we get, the more successful the event will be for everyone.

Cost and Registration

The cost for the weekend is $75. The cost includes:

  • Designated route and route sheets for 5 days.
  • Support on the designated routes.
  • A handful of other routes to be explored in the area.
  • Saturday night banquet at Garlinis including White Elephant gift exchange.
  • A group photo to commerate your weekend.
