Randonneuring Awards

Super Randonneur If you have completed a 200 km, 300 km, 400 km, and 600 km and are interested in applying for the Super Randonneur medal from Audax Club Parisien, please see http://www.rusa.org/superrand.html for an application. The application will ask for the ACP brevet numbers, which can be found under the "Brevet Results" section of the RUSA site (http://www.rusa.org/results_search.html).

1000 km to 5000 km RUSA is pleased to offer medal awards designed to encourage participation in domestic brevets put on by our hard working Regional Brevet Administrators (RBAs). The awards are for any RUSA member in good standing who compiles 1000 km, 2000 km, 3000 km, 4000 km, or 5000 km of RUSA events in one calendar year. More information available at http://www.rusa.org/rusaaward.html. Applications for the award are due by December 15.

Randonneur 5000 In 1961, the Audax Club Parisien created an award for those randonneurs who have completed 5000 km in BRM events. To qualify for this award, the successful randonneur must complete the full BRM series (200, 300, 400, 600, and 1000k), a Paris-Brest-Paris, a Flèche Vélocio and additional brevets to bring the total distance up to 5000 km. RUSA has further R5000 details.
