2002 100 km Populaire

This year's 100 km Populaire had a fabulous turnout of 75 riders and a gorgeous sunny and brisk day. According to Alex Taylor it was -1C and "Tres Populaire". Thanks to Mark Thomas for organizing it and Bill Dussler and Duane Wright for getting it listed as a Cascade ride. It was nice to see old friends and new faces, and this looks like a great year for SIR!
