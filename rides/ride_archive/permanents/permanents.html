While Mark Roehrig recovers from his injury, Mark Thomas will be handling the permanents on his behalf. Please contact Mark Thomas (rba@seattlerandonneur.org) to arrange rides. Send registration forms and completed cards to Mark Thomas, 13543 160th Avenue NE, Redmond, WA 98052.

SIR has a number of RUSA Permanents mapped out. For more information on the RUSA Permanents program, please consult the RUSA web site at http://www.rusa.org/permfaq.html. A Permanent is like a brevet but you can ride it any time, not just on one specific date. Your ride must be arranged in advance with the SIR Permanents Coordinator (Mark Roehrig at markjayr@earthlink.net). Two weeks advance notice may be required. To ride a permanent, submit the registration form and signed waiver to the Permanents Coordinator, who will then send you a control card.

SIR Rules for Permanents

  1. Registration and signed waiver must be provided to the Permanents Coordinator (Mark Roehrig at markjayr@earthlink.net) before the ride.
  2. Receipts for each control including start and finish shall be provided for each rider. Except Information Controls.
  3. All Brevet Cards must be returned to the Permanents Coordinator within ten (10) days of completing the ride.
  4. Point-to-Point rides can be ridden in either direction, but you must have the correct Brevet Card and Cue sheet.
  5. DNF’s must be reported to the Permanents Coordinator within 3 days of the ride, either by phone or email.
  6. The RUSA rules for riders applies to Permanent routes, please review the RUSA website: http://www.rusa.org/brvreg.html
  7. Two weeks advance notice may be required.
