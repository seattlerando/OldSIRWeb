2002 BMB

Here are some photos from BMB submitted by Peter McKay, taken by Diane Goodwin at the Ludlow control. Ludlow, in the local Vermont language means rain. It rained both times we were there, hence the wet weather gear.

Stan Reynolds - that was one long and wet descent. August 22.

Peter and Greg...Greg refuses to wear clothes...Peter wonders if he should find another place to sit... August 22.

Mark contemplating the blue cardboard bike near him. August 22.

Stan Reynolds & Mark Thomas happy to be dry under the porch roof. August 24.

Peter under the tarp and readying himself for the very long and wet climb out of Ludlow and the long cold descent waiting on the other side.

"Live long and prosper" or maybe "ride long and rejoice?"
